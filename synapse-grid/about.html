Our Story

Built by an NRI.
Built for Every NRI.

Synapse Grid was born from a personal experience — and a determination to make sure no NRI family goes through it alone.

The Beginning

The Call We Were Not Prepared For

In 2019, our founder Arjun Malhotra received a 3am call from a neighbour in Bangalore — his parents had fallen ill and there was no one to help. Arjun was in San Francisco. He had to book the next flight home, missing a critical week of work, because there was simply no system in place.

That experience sparked a mission: to build the infrastructure that every NRI family deserves — a reliable, trusted, local presence they could count on from anywhere in the world.

Today, Synapse Grid serves over 2,000 families across 18 Indian cities, with a network of 400+ verified professionals dedicated to one thing: your peace of mind.

Our Mission

To make the distance between NRI families and their India life irrelevant — through trustworthy, professional, and deeply human support services that feel personal, not transactional.

Our Vision

A world where every Indian living abroad can pursue their global ambitions without sacrificing the wellbeing of the family and assets they've left behind in India.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

These aren't just words on a wall — they're the principles every Synapse Grid team member lives by, every single day.

Trust First

Every professional in our network is background-verified. We earn trust through transparency and consistent delivery.

Family Like

We treat every client's family as we would our own. Compassion and care are non-negotiable in everything we do.

Responsive

Emergencies don't wait. Neither do we. Our 60-minute emergency response commitment is our promise — and we keep it.

Transparent

No hidden charges. No vague updates. Every action is documented, photographed, and reported back to you clearly.

Long-term

We build relationships, not transactions. Many of our clients have been with us since day one — and that means everything.

Deeply Local

Our team knows the local systems, languages, and nuances — we're Indian at heart and that's our greatest strength.
